# Vintage Dark Prompt Guidelines

Use this when generating UI copy, component styles, or visual prompts for the Vintage theme in dark mode.

## Core Identity

- Mood: candlelit study room, rich and calm, long-session friendly.
- Tone: confident, quiet, and reader-first.
- Avoid: pure-black voids, oversaturated highlights, and game-like chrome.

## Locked Color Roles

- Main1 (page background): `#1e1811`
- Main2 (panel background): `#2f261d`
- Support (elevated surfaces): `#644a34`
- Accent (actions/highlights): `#f2d4a7`
- Text: `#f5e8d4`

## Typography Direction

- Headings: refined serif for chapter-like emphasis.
- Body/UI: high-legibility sans with moderate weight.
- Keep paragraph line-height generous for low-light readability.

## Component Direction

- Panels: layered warm dark surfaces with visible separation.
- Actions: accent should feel like warm brass, not neon.
- Popups/tooltips: high readability, compact copy, subtle edge glow.

## Motion Direction

- Use smooth fades and glides (160-240ms).
- Favor opacity and slight translate; avoid flashy scale loops.
- Motion should feel deliberate and low-distraction.

## Prompt Scaffold

"Design a [component/screen] in the Inkling Vintage Dark theme. Use Main1 `#1e1811`, Main2 `#2f261d`, Support `#644a34`, Accent `#f2d4a7`, and Text `#f5e8d4`. Keep the aesthetic warm-dark and literary, with layered surfaces, restrained shadows, and clean readable typography (serif headings + sans body). Use subtle 160-240ms transitions only."
