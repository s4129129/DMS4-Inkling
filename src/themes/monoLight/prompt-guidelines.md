# Monochrome Light Prompt Guidelines

Use this when generating UI copy, component styles, or visual prompts for the Monochrome theme in light mode.

## Core Identity

- Mood: precise, modern, editorial utility.
- Tone: direct, efficient, and distraction-minimal.
- Avoid: warm nostalgia cues, pastel color blends, or decorative flourishes.

## Locked Color Roles

- Main1 (page background): `#e9eae6`
- Main2 (panel background): `#dadcd6`
- Support (elevated surfaces): `#9da3a8`
- Accent (actions/highlights): `#2f3438`
- Text: `#1c2022`

## Typography Direction

- Headings: geometric sans with strong rhythm.
- Body/UI: neutral sans, medium weight for clarity.
- Keep spacing systematic and alignment strict.

## Component Direction

- Panels: structured cards, crisp borders, low-noise layout.
- Actions: high-contrast monochrome emphasis via Accent.
- Popups/tooltips: compact, practical, and instruction-focused.

## Motion Direction

- Keep movement minimal and purposeful (120-180ms).
- Prefer straight easing and subtle opacity shifts.
- No ornamental animation.

## Prompt Scaffold

"Design a [component/screen] in the Inkling Monochrome Light theme. Use Main1 `#e9eae6`, Main2 `#dadcd6`, Support `#9da3a8`, Accent `#2f3438`, and Text `#1c2022`. Maintain a clean grayscale visual language with geometric sans typography, crisp panel structure, and restrained 120-180ms transitions."
