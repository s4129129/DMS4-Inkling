# Vintage Light Prompt Guidelines

Use this when generating UI copy, component styles, or visual prompts for the Vintage theme in light mode.

## Core Identity

- Mood: warm paper desk, calm and literary, premium but approachable.
- Tone: clear, human, and focused on reading momentum.
- Avoid: sterile SaaS visuals, neon accents, or heavy gloss effects.

## Locked Color Roles

- Main1 (page background): `#f8f2e7`
- Main2 (panel background): `#e5d9c5`
- Support (elevated surfaces): `#a7774d`
- Accent (actions/highlights): `#563826`
- Text: `#2d2119`

## Typography Direction

- Headings: serif with editorial character (Newsreader/Cormorant style).
- Body/UI: clean sans with strong readability (Manrope/Source Sans style).
- Contrast target: body text must stay high-contrast against Main1/Main2.

## Component Direction

- Panels: soft corners, paper-like surfaces, subtle borders.
- Actions: accent-driven buttons with clear hierarchy.
- Popups/tooltips: tactile card feel, concise copy, no technical jargon.

## Motion Direction

- Use short, soft ease transitions (140-220ms).
- Prefer gentle rise/fade over springy bounce.
- Keep attention on content, not animation.

## Prompt Scaffold

"Design a [component/screen] in the Inkling Vintage Light theme. Use Main1 `#f8f2e7` as the page base, Main2 `#e5d9c5` for panels, Support `#a7774d` for lifted surfaces, Accent `#563826` for actions, and Text `#2d2119` for primary copy. Keep typography editorial (serif headings, readable sans body), with restrained warm shadows and subtle 140-220ms transitions."
